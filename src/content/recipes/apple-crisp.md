## Notes

- Adapted from [Sally's Baking Addiction](https://sallysbakingaddiction.com/apple-crisp/#tasty-recipes-83519)
- A mix of tart and sweet apples works best.
- Great served warm with vanilla ice cream or caramel.

## Instructions

- Heat the oven to 350°F and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
- Toss all filling ingredients together in a large bowl, then spread the mixture evenly in the dish.
- For the topping, whisk the flour, brown sugar, and cinnamon together.
- Cut in the cold butter with a fork or pastry cutter until the mixture is crumbly, then stir in the oats.
- Sprinkle the topping over the apples.
- Bake for about 45 minutes, until the topping is golden and the apples are bubbling around the edges.
- Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
- Cover leftovers and refrigerate for up to 5 days.
